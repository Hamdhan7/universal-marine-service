Maritime Security Services

Maritime piracy is no strangers to the merchant ships. There have been many preventive measures taken over a period of time to reduce the threat of piracy. Providing security guards to the ships while passing through high risk areas is one of them.

We have been involved in marine security business for last three years and have access to good quality PMSCs, enabling us to provide a competitive quote to all our clients for security transits. The company focuses on the helping its clients by providing a diverse range of maritime security services including following:
  • Assisting third party security teams and security kits in embarkation / disembarkation at Colombo Port / Hambantota Port / Trincomale Port and Galle Port in Sri Lanka,
  • Undertaking security tasks for the vessels passing through HRA,
  • Providing vessels for security escort services,
  • Helping security companies in sourcing security equipment,
  • Hardening of the vessels passing through HRA,
  • Other Maritime Security Services.
